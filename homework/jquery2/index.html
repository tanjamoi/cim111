Shibuya Crossing

Rumoured to be the busiest intersection in the world (and definitely in Japan), Shibuya Crossing is like a giant beating heart, sending people in all directions with every pulsing light change. Perhaps nowhere else says ‘Welcome to Tokyo’ better than this.

Check out Tokyo’s most visited temple. The entrance to the temple complex is via the fantastic, red Kaminari-mon (雷門; Thunder Gate) and busy shopping street Nakamise-dōri.
